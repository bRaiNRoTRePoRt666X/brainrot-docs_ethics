# Brainrot Glossary (for normies and ethics auditors)

This glossary supports our AI ethics documentation with transparency and consent in mind. Do no harm when using these terms.

- **Skibidi**: Noun/verb/adjective. Context-dependent chaos.  
- **Rizz**: Charisma. Can be negative ("negative rizz").  
- **Sigma**: Lone wolf who reads ethics docs.  
- **Ohio**: Synonym for final boss of cursed energy.  
- **Touch Grass**: Mandatory intervention command. Part of our sustainability clause.  
- **Brainrot**: The condition this entire repo exists to manage responsibly. See Hall of Shame for worst cases.  
- **AI Ethics**: The framework governing all AI-generated content in this repo. Consent required.  
- **Consent**: Permission from all parties before featuring them in content. Non-negotiable.  
- **Transparency**: Being open about AI use, sources, and intentions. Core principle.

Add new terms via PR. Keep it factual.
