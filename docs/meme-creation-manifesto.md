# Meme Creation Manifesto

**How to cook brainrot that doesn't rot society.**

This manifesto upholds consent, transparency, and our core AI ethics principles.

1. **Originality First**  
   Remix, don't rip. Credit the original cook. Transparency about sources is non-negotiable.

2. **Punch Up, Not Down**  
   Mock power, not the powerless. Do no harm to vulnerable groups.

3. **Dopamine with a Side of Value**  
   Make them laugh, then make them think (or at least touch grass).

4. **Format Responsibly**  
   No 0.5-second vertical video that auto-plays harmful content on mute. Sustainability of attention matters.

5. **Community First**  
   If your meme starts a cult, include an off-switch. Violators go to the Hall of Shame.

We create to entertain. Not to end civilization.
