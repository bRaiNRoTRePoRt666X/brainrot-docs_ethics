# Reporting Standards

**How to document brainrot incidents without becoming the problem.**

These standards align with our AI ethics framework and do no harm principle.

- **Fact-Check Level:** Mandatory before posting.  
- **Source Transparency:** Link every claim. Transparency builds trust.  
- **Bias Disclosure:** "I'm terminally online, here's my bias."  
- **Victim Consent:** Never name victims without explicit permission. Consent is non-negotiable.  
- **Resolution Path:** Every report must end with "what should happen next" (not just toxic responses).  
- **Sustainability:** Include "touch grass" reminders. Don't doomscroll your audience.

Bad reporting goes straight to the Hall of Shame.
